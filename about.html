About Roland

✦

A spiritual, empathic, and bad bitch all in one, Queen Empress Roland blends elegance with slay to create a musical experience like no other. Inspired by a deep fascination with royal history, the name represents the freedom to be whoever you want to be — even a Queen and an Empress at the same time.

"Through music, I hope to inspire others to embrace their own path, take things step by step, and never compare themselves to anyone else."

Having started music in 2015, Queen Empress Roland has developed a sound that is as flamboyant, wacky, and unpredictable as their personality. The music swings between high-energy party anthems, deeply emotional ballads, and dreamy storytelling — all tied together by thoughtful lyrics and personal reflections.

On stage, Roland is a magnet of energy and charisma. Whether leading the crowd into a euphoric dance or delivering raw, emotional performances — the goal is always the same: to make people feel at ease, fully engaged, and swept away by the moment.

As for the future? Sharing my art and connect with people, of course. With dreams of collaborating with Dua Lipa on a massive party anthem and getting sung off the track by Mariah Carey, Roland is set on making waves in the music industry. The ultimate goal? Headlining major festivals and owning the stage like the true royalty they are.
